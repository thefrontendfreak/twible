Registeration, Verification and Profile Setup

1.1 How do I Install it?

To install, go to https://comeratech.com/, Tap on Install Tab.

Android users can install from Google Play. https://play.google.com/store/apps/twibleid7872

iOS users can install from the App Store.

https://apps.apple.com/ae/app/twible/id1462023656

1.2 How do I Configure the app?

After downloading the app:

  • Tap the Open tab.
  • Read the Term and Conditions and Privacy policy carefully then Tap on Agree and Continue.
  • Select your country.
  • Enter your phone number. You will receive an OTP on your phone.
  • Allow the contacts & phone call permissions.
  • Setup profile with profile picture and profile name to start using it.
1.3 How do I verify my number?

Twible verifies your number through OTP (One-time Password) at the time of registering for the app or when you change the number.
